Move Management

Our team will develop a unique solution in line with your business needs and are equipped to transport large heavy items such as hardware, computer systems and office technologies by using the correct tools to transport these items efficiently without damage and delay.

We are Fully Insured

We are fully insured against liability damages which offer our clients piece of mind by ensuring that your property is covered in the event of damages.

This part of the service is not limited to your belongings, but provides cover in the event of damage to vertical surfaces such as walls, doors and glass panels. Such items are taken extra care of by using protective covers and lightweight core flute panels which are taped in place using a low adhesive masking tape. Doors and frames can be protected with custom made padded protectors held in place by spring clips to ensure unobstructed use of the doorway at all times.
